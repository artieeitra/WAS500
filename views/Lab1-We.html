Book list

Title: "We"
Author: Yevgeny Zamyatin
Publishing Date: 1924
Publisher: E. P. Dutton
About the book: A masterwork of wit and dark humour that perfectly foresaw the horrors of Stalinism,
Yevgeny Zamyatin's page-turning science fiction adventure, The iconic dystopian novel "We" served
as the inspiration for works by Aldous Huxley, George Orwell, Margaret Atwood, and many more.
At the start of the twenty-first century, as at the start of the twentieth, its message
of hope and caution is still relevant.
